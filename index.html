Motivation — Visual Evidence

Visual comparison of waveform, mel-spectrogram, and spectral error maps between clean and watermarked audio. The spectral error maps (bottom row) reveal that different watermarking methods induce distinct, structured distortion patterns in the time-frequency domain.
